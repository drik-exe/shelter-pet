Become a volunteer

Volunteers (from the French “volontaire” - volunteer) are active people who provide all possible assistance on a voluntary and free basis. Today, the protection and conservation of animals in the Republic of Belarus without volunteers is simply unthinkable. Volunteer work is an invaluable contribution to this good cause. You can become a member of a permanent volunteer group while living in Minsk. In other cities, you can participate in OZH activities interactively via the Internet.
